# Hotel Revenue Management: Simultaneous Overbooking and Allocation

This research introduces a novel approach to hotel revenue management by simultaneously optimizing overbooking and room allocation decisions within the price optimization framework.

**Published in:** Computers & Industrial Engineering, Volume 137, Pages 106073 (2019)  
**DOI:** [10.1016/j.cie.2019.106073](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cie.2019.106073)  
**Citations:** 34

## Key Contributions

- Development of integrated mathematical models for simultaneous overbooking and allocation decisions
- Demonstration of revenue improvements through coordinated decision-making
- Risk management strategies for hospitality industry pricing

## Methodology

Our approach combines optimization techniques with revenue management principles to create a unified framework for hotel pricing decisions.

## Results

The simultaneous approach shows significant revenue improvements compared to traditional sequential decision-making processes in hotel revenue management.

# Hotel Revenue Management for the Transient Segment

This research develops a comprehensive taxonomy for hotel revenue management with a specific focus on the transient customer segment.

**Published in:** International Journal of Contemporary Hospitality Management, Volume 32, Issue 1, Pages 108-125 (2020)  
**DOI:** [10.1108/IJCHM-01-2019-0065](https://doi.org/10.1108/IJCHM-01-2019-0065)  
**Citations:** 16

## Research Methodology

- Systematic literature review of hotel revenue management practices
- Development of taxonomy framework
- Expert validation of classification system

## Taxonomy Development

Our taxonomy provides a structured framework for understanding revenue management practices specifically tailored to transient hotel guests.

## Research Contributions

- First comprehensive taxonomy for transient segment revenue management
- Identification of research gaps in hospitality management
- Framework for future research directions

## Practical Applications

The taxonomy serves as a guide for hotel managers to systematically approach revenue management for transient customers.

# Evaluation of Bid Price and Network Revenue Management Methods

This research provides a comprehensive comparison of bid price and nested network revenue management allocation methods across various market conditions.

**Published in:** Computers & Industrial Engineering, Volume 115, Pages 100-108 (2018)  
**DOI:** [10.1016/j.cie.2017.10.026](https://doi.org/10.1016/j.cie.2017.10.026)  
**Citations:** 21

## Research Objectives

- Compare bid price and nested allocation methods
- Evaluate performance under different demand patterns
- Identify optimal strategies for network revenue management

## Key Findings

Our analysis reveals significant differences in performance between bid price and nested methods depending on market conditions and demand characteristics.

## Implications

The results provide practical guidance for revenue managers in selecting appropriate allocation methods based on their specific operating environment.
